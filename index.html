About Us

Founded in 2007 as one of the branch enterprises of JAVA GROUP, TIANCHANG HENGSHENG MEDICAL DEVICES CO., LTD. specializes in manufacturing a variety of medical surgical gloves and examination gloves.
Our own brand X-Glove and OEM brands designated by customers are produced using advanced manufacturing technologies to ensure the highest quality.
In addition to gloves, we also produce condoms, skin staplers, vaginal speculums, and other disposable medical products, providing a comprehensive range of healthcare solutions.

Reach

Our products are not limited to the domestic market but are exported globally, including to the USA, Germany, the Netherlands, Turkey, Russia, Brazil, among others. Our reputation for quality and reliability has made us a preferred healthcare partner worldwide.

Quality

We hold a medical equipment production license issued by the Food and Drug Administration of Anhui Province, People's Republic of China, and are ISO13485 and CE certified by TÜV. These achievements speak to our dedication to product quality and safety.

Innovation

Embracing innovation is at the heart of our mission. We continually invest in research and development to improve our products, ensuring they meet the evolving needs of healthcare professionals and patients worldwide.

Mission

We aim to make X-Glove a globally recognized brand in the medical glove industry through unity, cooperation, and relentless dedication. We are committed to innovation, excellence, and enhancing our product quality and services to ensure the health and safety of everyone.
